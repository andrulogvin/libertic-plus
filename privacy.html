Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 24, 2026

Welcome to Libertic Plus: Habit Breaker ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our app.

1. Data We Collect

Libertic Plus is designed with privacy as a priority. We collect minimal data necessary for the app's functionality:

  • Email Address: Collected during voluntary registration to allow you to log in and sync your data across devices.
  • Habit Data: We store your cigarette count logs and timer data locally on your device and securely on our servers to provide you with statistics and progress tracking.
  • App Usage Data: We may collect anonymous diagnostic data to improve app performance (e.g., crash logs).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide the core functionality of the App (timer, logging, statistics).
  • Send daily motivational notifications (if enabled).
  • Respond to support requests.

3. Data Protection and User Rights (GDPR/UK GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: You can see your data within the App.
  • Right to Correction: You can update your settings and logs.
  • Right to Erasure (Delete Data): You can use the "Delete All My Data" button in the Settings screen to permanently remove all your data from our servers and your device.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request an export of your data.

4. No Tracking Policy

Libertic Plus does not use third-party tracking pixels or behavioral advertising. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Encryption Compliance

The App uses standard encryption (HTTPS/SSL) for all data transmission between your device and our servers. We do not use any non-exempt encryption technology.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Support Page Feedback Form.
