Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 08, 2026

Libertic Plus ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Libertic Plus.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. All your quit journey data, including quit date, cigarette consumption, and savings, is stored locally on your device in protected storage. We do not have access to this data.

2. App Usage Data

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools. Your usage of the app remains private and anonymous.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Libertic Plus does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 17.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
