Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my statistics?

You can reset your timer by tapping "I Relapsed" on the Dashboard. To delete all data, go to Settings > "Delete All My Data".

Is my data private?

Yes. All your habit data is stored locally on your device. We do not track personal behavior.

Notifications not working?

Check your device settings to ensure Libertic Plus has permission to send alerts.
