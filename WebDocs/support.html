FAQ

How do I reset statistics?

Tap "I Relapsed" on dashboard to reset timer. To delete all data, go to Settings > "Delete All My Data".

Is my data private?

Yes. Data is stored securely on your device. We do not track personal behavior for ads.

Notifications not working?

Check device settings ensuring Libertic Plus has permission. Toggle notifications in App Settings.
