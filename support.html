Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my statistics?

You can reset your current habit timer by tapping the "I Relapsed" (Я сорвался) button on the Dashboard. To completely clear all statistics and data, go to the Settings screen and use the "Reset Statistics" or "Delete All My Data" options.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your habit logs are stored securely and we do not track your behavior for advertising purposes. You can find more details in our Privacy Policy.

I stopped receiving notifications.

Please check your device's notification settings to ensure Libertic Plus has permission to send alerts. You can also toggle the notification setting off and on again within the App's Settings screen.
